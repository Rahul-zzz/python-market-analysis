# Market Data Analysis

## Overview

This project analyzes job market data collected through Selenium-based web scraping to identify workforce demand across the logistics, automotive, and skilling sectors. It filters raw job postings into structured categories and generates market insights that support workforce training and enablement initiatives.

## Objectives

* Organize scraped job market data into meaningful workforce categories.
* Analyze hiring trends across logistics, automotive, and skilling domains.
* Generate structured datasets for market research.
* Support evidence-based workforce training and enablement decisions.

## Features

* Filters job postings into:

  * Logistics Workforce
  * Automotive Workforce
  * Skilling Programs
* Automatically generates a structured Excel workbook.
* Analyzes hiring trends, job roles, locations, companies, and required skills.
* Produces market evidence to support workforce analysis.

## Technologies Used

* Python
* Pandas
* OpenPyXL
* Selenium (for data collection)

* Common job roles
