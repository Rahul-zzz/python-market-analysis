RESEARCH LOGISTICS CERTIFICATIONS & TRAINING PROGRAMS IN INDIA
    Research on logistics workforce development in India identified several formal training and 
    certification programs supported by the National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) and the 
    Logistics Sector Skill Council (LSC). These programs cover areas such as warehouse operations, 
    inventory management, supply chain management, transportation operations, fleet management, and 
    last-mile delivery services. Industry-recognized certifications help improve employability, establish 
    standardized operational knowledge, and support workforce readiness for logistics and supply chain 
    roles across India.
KEY FINDINGS:
    The most significant finding from this week's logistics job market analysis is that employers place 
    greater emphasis on operational and logistics-specific skills than on formal certifications or language 
    requirements. Training opportunities and fresher-focused hiring were rarely mentioned, indicating a 
    preference for candidates who already possess relevant industry experience. Additionally, core 
    logistics skills such as dispatch, operations, warehouse management, transportation, and supply chain 
    knowledge consistently appeared across job postings, highlighting their importance in the sector.
